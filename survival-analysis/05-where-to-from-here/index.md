At this stage, we hope that you have successfully completed this tutorial! This concludes our introductory module on survival analysis and biomarker discovery.

While we have covered the fundamental methods of survival analysis and biomarker discovery, there is much more to explore! As discussed by Clark et al. (2003a, 2003b) and Bradburn et al. (2003a, 2003b), simple comparisons can be misleading if they don't account for other factors. For instance, if one treatment group happens to be younger than another, a simple survival analysis might falsely attribute better outcomes to the treatment rather than age.
